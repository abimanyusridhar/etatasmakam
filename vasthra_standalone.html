✨ Our Story

A Cleaner Solution,
Born from Need.

From a simple idea to a city-wide laundry platform.

We started Vasthra after noticing how difficult it was to find reliable, affordable laundry services in Bengaluru. Dropping clothes yourself, not knowing the cost upfront, missing delivery timelines — all frustrating.

Vasthra works just like Swiggy — but instead of connecting you to restaurants, we connect you to trusted local laundry shops. Simple booking, transparent pricing, WhatsApp updates every step of the way.

  • Pickup and delivery from your doorstep
  • Transparent per-cloth pricing, no surprises
  • WhatsApp updates at every stage
  • Trusted local laundry partner shops
  • Customer satisfaction guaranteed
